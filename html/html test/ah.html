WIZKID

ABOUT

Wizkid A Nigerian who began singing since 11 is a self made Afropop modern musician. He recently released an album "Made in Lagos" which was quite sensational and spent several months on top charts. Wizzy is set to release a new EP after his first "SFTOS" this year July.

He is set to work with various foreign based artists as well as African contemporaries.
